OUR STORY

Plane is a multidisciplinary creative studio. Together, we design, develop, and produce work for people in whose values we firmly believe. We can be hired for a variety of assignments, projects, and gigs across a wide range of creative fields.

Zach and Josh met in the winter of 2011 while working as members at ADX, Portland's Maker space, where they discovered a mutual love of art, punk, and burritos. In a space they constructed over the shop's kitchen, they founded Band with the aid of their ADX pals, a diversified, handmade, collaborative creative workshop.
